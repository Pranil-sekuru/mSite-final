 Passive Sensing No Self-Report Required The system detects social isolation using conversation sensors, GPS, accelerometers, and phone usage — without requiring any active input from the user. Context-Aware Triggers Right Time, Right Place When the system detects you're alone and away from home, it fires a personalized CBT-based intervention prompt — not on a schedule, but when it matters.
